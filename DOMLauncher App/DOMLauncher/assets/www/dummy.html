Well hello there!

You are seeing me because of a few possibilities. This could be the first time you launched the app and did not copy the 'DOMLauncher' folder to your internal or external sdcard. Second is that there is an error in the choosen DOMod to load. Check your 'config.txt' within the DOMLauncher folder. Lastly, it could be that the external sdcard is mounted and inaccessable by the device and your files are stored on the external sdcard. Make sure your sdcard is readable. It is preferable to store the DOMlauncher folder on the internal sdcard to avoid this issue.
